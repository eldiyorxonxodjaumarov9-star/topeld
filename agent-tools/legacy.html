    Frequently Asked Questions

    Is your ELD FMCSA certified?
    In compliance with the FMCSA’s ELD Mandate, our ELD Compliance Solution was registered and certified by the FMCSA.


    Do I have to print my logs during an inspection?
    With our ELD, you’re not required to present physical logs. Our ELD is able to transfer data electronically, but screen displays can also be presented if need be. However, an enforcement officer may request that additional information be provided by email, fax, or other similar means within 48 hours of the roadside inspection.

    Will your ELD help prevent HOS violations?
    Legacy ELD is designed to notify drivers, safety officials of potential violations beforehand. Notifications will be received by the aforementioned parties 1 hour, 30 minutes, 15 minutes, and 5 minutes prior to the violation.

    How can drivers create driver vehicle inspection reports (DVIRs)?
    Drivers can use the Legacy ELD Electronic Logbook App to create DVIRs. The DVIRs created in the app can be accessed by fleet managers by viewing the Legacy ELD Portal (Dashboard).

    How is driving time recorded?
    A driver’s driving time will be automatically recorded as long as the driver connects their mobile device to the ELD via Bluetooth and the vehicle is registered in the Legacy ELD App. A vehicle is considered to be in motion if it is traveling over 5 mph.
